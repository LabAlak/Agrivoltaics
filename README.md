# 🌱 Agrivoltaic System Optimization

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on the design and optimization of an agrivoltaic system in Montpellier (France), combining photovoltaic energy production with agricultural constraints.

The objective is to maximize energy production while considering crop requirements such as shading, temperature, and water management.

---

## ⚙️ Methodology

The project was developed using Python and the pvlib library to simulate photovoltaic system performance.

### Key components:
- Solar position modeling (zenith & azimuth angles)
- Irradiance and energy yield calculation
- PV module and inverter modeling (SAPM model)
- Simulation of shadow area under panels

---

## 📊 Key Results

### 🔋 Energy Production
- Montpellier identified as optimal location
- Strong seasonal patterns in solar production

### ☀️ Fixed vs Tracking System
- Single-axis tracker increases energy production by **~35%**
- Extends production window (morning & evening)

### 🌑 Shadow Analysis
- Shadow area increases with tilt angle:
  - 0° → 2.36 m²
  - 90° → 5.97 m²
- Trade-off between:
  - energy production
  - agricultural performance

---

## 🌱 Agrivoltaic Insight

The project highlights the importance of balancing:
- solar energy generation
- crop growth conditions

Shading plays a critical role in:
- photosynthesis
- soil moisture
- crop yield
